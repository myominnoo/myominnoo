abstract: Bhutan is currently facing a double burden of non-communicable (NCDs) and
  communicable diseases, with rising trends of NCDs. The 2014 STEPS survey identified
  high prevalence of several NCD risk factors; however, associations with socio-demographic
  characteristics as well as clustering of risk factors were not assessed. This study
  aimed to determine the distribution and clustering of modifiable NCD risk factors
  among adults in Bhutan and their demographic and social determinants.
